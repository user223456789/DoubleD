The Process

Simple, structured, well-paid

STEP 01 — APPLY
Create your profile
Submit your credentials, research focus, and domain expertise. We verify your affiliation and add you to the network within 48 hours.
STEP 02 — MATCH
Get matched to a review
When a firm posts a request in your domain, you'll receive a notification. You can accept or decline with no obligation. NDAs are pre-generated and auto-signed.
STEP 03 — REVIEW
Use our structured template
No blank page. Our 13-section Cleantech Technical Efficacy Report template guides your assessment — TRL, scientific validity, scalability, IP, team credibility, and risk.
STEP 04 — GET PAID
Automatic payment
Submit your completed report. Once accepted by the firm (typically within 24 hours), payment hits your account automatically. No invoicing, no chasing.

Eligibility

Who we're looking for

01
Academic Affiliation
Current appointment at an Australian university or public research institution (CSIRO, ANSTO, etc.).
02
Domain Expertise
PhD-level knowledge in a relevant cleantech domain: energy storage, solar, hydrogen, carbon capture, advanced materials, grid tech.
